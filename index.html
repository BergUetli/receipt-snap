You can skip this. The app already reads receipts by itself, free of charge. If you have your own Google AI key, pasting it here makes the reading more accurate. The key stays on your phone.
Tip: in your browser, open the menu (the three dots or the share button) and tap Add to Home Screen. This puts Receipt Snap on your phone like a normal app.
Please note: This app helps you keep records. It does not give tax or financial advice. The camera can sometimes read a receipt wrong, so please check every entry before you save it. Keep your paper receipts, and save your Excel file from time to time. Saved files stay safely on your phone. If your browser's saved data is ever cleared, only the list inside this app is emptied. Your saved files are not touched, and you can bring one back with the button 'Load entries from a saved file'.
The very first scan takes a little longer while the reader gets ready. For best results: lay the receipt flat, use good light, and hold the camera close.
